About Worm Escape

Worm Escape is a wave-based arcade game where you defend against worms, roaches, lizards, frogs, and massive bosses rising from the sewage below. Tap every creature before it escapes — one miss costs a point of health. Survive 20 handcrafted levels, brave endless mode, and chase 15+ medals.

Requirements

  • iOS / iPadOS: 18.1 or later
  • macOS: 15.2 or later
  • tvOS: 18.2 or later
  • Internet connection: required for Game Center leaderboards and rewarded ads

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my ad-free purchase?

Open the game → tap Rewards on the main menu → tap Restore Purchases. Your ad-free status will sync from your Apple ID across all devices.

I purchased the 50× Powerups bundle but didn't get my powerups.

Close and reopen the app — powerups are credited on the next launch if the transaction was still finalizing. If the issue persists, email wormescape@gmail.com with your Apple ID receipt.

My Game Center scores aren't showing up.

Make sure you're signed into Game Center: Settings → Game Center on iOS, or System Settings → Game Center on macOS. Then open the Leaderboard screen in Worm Escape to sync your scores.

How do I reset my game progress?

Delete the app and reinstall it. Local progress (high scores, levels unlocked, medals earned) will be wiped. Note: your Game Center leaderboard rank is stored by Apple and cannot be erased by the app.

How do rewarded ads work?

After a failed run, you can optionally watch a short ad to revive your character or retry the level. Ads are never required — they're always optional. Purchase "Go Ad-Free" from the Rewards screen to skip ads permanently.

Can I play offline?

Yes — all core gameplay works offline. Only the Leaderboard screen and rewarded ads require an internet connection.

Why does the app ask for tracking permission?

If you grant tracking permission, we show personalized ads via Google AdMob. Declining means you'll still see ads, but they will be non-personalized. Either choice is fine.
